Bio

I was born in Birmingham, England in 1994. I am the eldest of five girls.
I studied Economics at the University of Leeds before becoming a Maths teacher. I then moved to the Civil Service where I have worked in three government departments: MHCLG, Defra and most recently the DfE. I currently work as a Senior Strategy Advisor, but I am hoping to transition careers into software engineering.
